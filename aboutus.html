CORPORATE

Founded in the year 2000, PT Fourangle Indomoto, serving as the key player behind the VAIR Motor Indonesia brand, is dedicated to positioning itself as a prominent manufacturer of two-wheelers and commercial vehicles within the Indonesian market. This commitment is underscored by the company's unwavering focus on delivering high-quality VAIR motorcycles at affordable prices to cater to the diverse needs of the Indonesian populace.

Situated in Surakarta, the expansive 20-hectare production facility of PT Fourangle Indomoto stands as a testament to its manufacturing prowess. With a production capacity of up to 1000 units per day, it proudly ranks among the largest automotive factories in Indonesia. Beyond sheer production, the company is actively fostering the growth of domestic component industries, solidifying its role in the VAIR supply chain.

For the convenience of the public, PT Fourangle Indomoto has established an extensive network of over 700 service points, strategically dispersed across various districts, with continuous expansion on the horizon. Moreover, to facilitate widespread access to VAIR Motor products, the company has forged partnerships with renowned financial institutions, including ADIRA Finance, BCA Multifinance, MEGA Finance, and others.

VAIR BRAND

VAIR is a prominent player in the Indonesian automotive industry, specifically focusing on the motorcycle sector. Established with a commitment to innovation, quality, and a passion for two-wheeled mobility, VAIR has carved a niche for itself in the dynamic and competitive Indonesian market. The brand's name, derived from the combination of "Vehicle" and "Air," reflects its dedication to providing cutting-edge and exhilarating riding experiences.

VAIR boasts an impressive product lineup that caters to the diverse needs and preferences of motorcycle enthusiasts in Indonesia. From sleek and stylish scooters designed for urban commuting to robust and powerful motorcycles tailored for adventurous off-road journeys, VAIR offers a comprehensive range of options. The brand places a strong emphasis on incorporating advanced technology, ergonomic design, and fuel efficiency into its motorcycles, ensuring that riders not only enjoy the thrill of the road but also benefit from state-of-the-art engineering.

Vision of VAIR

To be the foremost trailblazer in the motorcycle automotive industry in Indonesia, VAIR is committed to leading with innovation that guides towards a unique riding experience, empowers mobility, and resonates with motorcycle enthusiasts nationwide.

Mission of VAIR

  1. Limitless Innovation: Pioneer groundbreaking technology to create motorcycles that redefine the riding experience.
  2. Forefront Quality: Prioritize top-tier quality for reliable and enduring motorcycles that instill pride in every owner.
  3. Green Practices: Champion eco-friendly production, reducing environmental impact for a sustainable future.
  4. Community Unity: Cultivate a strong riding community, fostering connections and shared passion among enthusiasts.

Commitment to Sustainability

In alignment with the global shift towards sustainability, VAIR is committed to reducing its environmental footprint. The brand invests in research and development to introduce eco-friendly technologies and materials in its manufacturing processes. VAIR motorcycles are designed to meet stringent emission standards while maintaining optimal performance. By prioritizing sustainability, VAIR not only contributes to the well-being of the planet but also resonates with environmentally conscious consumers who seek responsible choices in their automotive investments

Community Engagement and Support

Beyond its products, VAIR actively engages with the motorcycle community in Indonesia. The brand organizes events, rallies, and rider meet-ups to foster a sense of camaraderie among enthusiasts. VAIR also provides comprehensive after-sales support, including maintenance services and readily available spare parts. This commitment to customer satisfaction ensures that VAIR not only sells motorcycles but also establishes lasting relationships with its customers, creating a loyal community of riders who proudly identify with the brand. VAIR's presence extends beyond the showroom, creating a holistic experience that goes hand in hand with the thrill of riding.
